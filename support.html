Frequently asked

How do I time a contraction?

Tap the big button when a contraction starts, and tap it again when it ends. The App records the duration and the interval since the last one, and shows your pattern on the timeline.

What does the 5-1-1 prompt mean?

5-1-1 is a commonly referenced general guideline (contractions about 5 minutes apart, lasting about 1 minute, for about 1 hour). It is educational information only, not medical advice. Always follow your healthcare provider’s instructions.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the paywall in the App and tap “Restore.” Purchases are tied to your Apple ID, so they restore on any device signed in with the same account.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID: on your iPhone, open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions. You can cancel there at any time.

Where is my data stored?

Your contraction history, checklist, and contacts are stored locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
